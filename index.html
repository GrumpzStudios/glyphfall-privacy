Glyphfall — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Glyphfall is a single-player puzzle roguelike game developed by Grumpz Sports Cards. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects (very little) and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Glyphfall does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All game data — including your progress, settings, cosmetics, and leaderboard — is stored locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting technology. We do not track your behavior, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

In-App Purchases

Glyphfall offers optional in-app purchases for cosmetic items and premium currency (gems). These transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's or Google's privacy policies for details on how they handle payment data.

Internet Access

The app requires minimal internet access solely for loading a web font (Google Fonts) and processing in-app purchases through the platform store. No personal data is transmitted during these operations.

Children's Privacy

Glyphfall does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party services used are:

Google Fonts — Used to load the "Press Start 2P" display font. Google's privacy policy applies to this font loading request. No personal data is sent beyond a standard HTTP request.

Apple App Store / Google Play Store — Used to process in-app purchases. Their respective privacy policies govern payment processing.

Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all game data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
